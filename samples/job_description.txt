From Kick-Start to Real Impact – What is STADA+?

STADA+ is more than an internship task.

It’s your opportunity to work on real business challenges with real impact.

 

As a STADA+ intern, you work exclusively on one dedicated business case. You take ownership, collaborate with experts across functions and help transform ideas into tangible outcomes – not just slides, but initiatives that move STADA forward. Learn more about our STADA+ program here.

 

Your STADA+ Business Case

In this STADA+ case, you will support the development and rollout of a harmonized pricing tool with the objective of creating a single source of truth for pricing data across STADA Europe.

The initiative focuses on simplifying fragmented pricing data, automating reporting processes and enabling faster, more reliable pricing decisions across markets – laying the foundation for future global scalability.

 

Your focus will be on:

Supporting the harmonization of pricing data across countries, systems and Excel-based sources
Contributing to the design and implementation of a centralized pricing table and reporting logic
Analyzing pricing data to improve transparency, governance and decision-making
Supporting pilot implementation and rollout preparation across European markets
Helping to identify efficiency gains, risk reduction and business impact from improved pricing processes
 

Case Owners: Altinok Gürtay, Head of IT Regulatory & Sebastian Thelen, Senior Director Global Supply Planning E2E Planning Excellence

Who We Are Looking For

 

You are currently enrolled in an advanced stage of your Bachelor’s degree or enrolled in a Master’s program
Your background is in Business Administration, Economics, Data Science, Computer Science, Business Informatics or a related field
You bring either a strong data-focused or process-focused profile, ideally with an interest in pricing, commercial or enterprise-wide data topics:
Data focus: You enjoy working with data models, structured datasets and reporting logic, and are interested in supporting the design or enhancement of a harmonized pricing data model
Process focus: You have a strong interest in analyzing, documenting and structuring business processes and supporting organizational change initiatives
You are comfortable working with SQL queries, Snowflake, Data Bricks or similar and building reports in Power BI, but can also still process data in more traditional tools like Excel
You work structured, analytical and solution-oriented, even in complex environments
You are curious, proactive and enjoy collaborating with cross-functional and international stakeholders
