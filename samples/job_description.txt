Senior Data Scientist – ACME Corp
Location: Remote (EU timezone preferred)

Type: Full-time

About the Role:
We're looking for a Senior Data Scientist to join our Core Analytics team. You will lead the development of end-to-end machine learning solutions that drive product innovation and business growth. You’ll be responsible for everything from exploratory data analysis and feature engineering to deploying scalable models in production and mentoring junior team members.

Requirements:

5+ years of experience in data science, machine learning, or predictive modeling

Deep expertise in Python and the PyData stack (Pandas, Scikit-learn, NumPy)

Strong experience with Deep Learning frameworks (PyTorch or TensorFlow)

Proficiency in SQL and working with large-scale data warehouses (Snowflake, BigQuery)

Solid understanding of statistical modeling, experimental design (A/B testing), and causal inference

Experience deploying models into production environments using Docker or Kubernetes

Advanced degree (M.S. or Ph.D.) in a quantitative field (Math, CS, Physics, or Statistics)

Nice to Have:

Experience with MLOps tools (MLflow, Kubeflow, or SageMaker)

Familiarity with NLP (Large Language Models) or Computer Vision

Contributions to open-source data science libraries

Experience with real-time stream processing (Kafka, Flink)

What You’ll Do:

Design, build, and maintain predictive models that impact core business KPIs

Lead the "data-first" strategy for product features from ideation to launch

Build automated data pipelines and feature stores to support model training

Communicate complex technical findings to non-technical stakeholders to drive decision-making

Conduct rigorous code reviews and mentor junior data scientists on best practices

Collaborate with Data Engineering to optimize model performance and scalability in the cloud
